Condition Glossary

  • Emphysema: A lung condition involving damage to the air sacs.
  • Fibrosis: Thickening or scarring of lung tissue.
  • Fracture: A break in a bone, possibly rib or spine in the image.
  • Infiltration: Presence of foreign substances (fluid or cells) in the lungs.
  • Lung Opacity: An area that appears white or cloudy, indicating a possible abnormality.
  • Mass: A larger abnormal growth, possibly tumor or inflammation.
  • Nodule: A small round mass that can be benign or malignant.
